# Share & Collaboration Features - Implementation Summary

## ✅ Enhancement #5: Completed for Research Intelligence Hub

### New Features Added

#### 1. Share Button with Dropdown Menu
Located next to Export button in the action buttons section.

#### 2. Three Share Options:

**a) Copy Full Analysis**
- Copies complete research analysis to clipboard
- Includes: Topic, Overall Assessment, Papers, Research Gaps
- Format: Plain text, easy to paste anywhere
- Use case: Share in documents, emails, or messaging apps

**b) Copy Summary (Shareable Text)**
- Generates a concise, social-media-friendly summary
- Includes emoji indicators (📊 📚 🔍 🚀)
- Shows counts: papers found, research gaps, future directions
- Perfect for: Twitter, LinkedIn, Slack, Teams

**c) Share via Email**
- Opens default email client with pre-filled content
- Subject: "Research Analysis: [Topic]"
- Body: Includes topic, assessment, and attribution
- One-click sharing with colleagues

### UI/UX Details

**Share Button:**
- Icon: Share/network icon

**Share Menu:**

## Usage Instructions

1. Generate research analysis
2. Click "Share" button
3. Choose sharing method:
   - **Copy Full Analysis**: For detailed sharing
   - **Copy Summary**: For quick social media posts
   - **Share via Email**: For professional communication
4. Content is copied/email opens automatically
5. Paste or send as needed
